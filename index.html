Our Story

Low & Slow Since Forever

Where BBQ is Done Right

At The Barbeque Pit, we believe great barbecue can't be rushed. Our meats are smoked low and slow over real wood until they're fall-off-the-bone tender with that perfect smoke ring you crave.

Located in the heart of Portland, TN, we've been serving up authentic Southern BBQ to locals and travelers alike. Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch or feeding the whole family, we've got you covered.
