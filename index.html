Welcome to The Pit

At The Barbeque Pit, we believe great BBQ can't be rushed. Our meats are smoked low and slow over real wood until they're fall-off-the-bone tender with that perfect smoke ring you crave.

Located in the heart of Portland, TN, we've been serving up authentic Southern BBQ to locals and travelers alike. Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch, feeding the whole family, or planning your next big event we've got you covered.
